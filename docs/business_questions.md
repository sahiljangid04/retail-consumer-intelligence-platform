
Business Questions

This document contains the key business questions that will guide the entire analytics project.

⸻

Executive Dashboard

* What is the total revenue?
* How many orders have been placed?
* How many customers have made purchases?
* What is the average order value?
* How has revenue changed over time?

⸻

Sales Analytics

* Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
* Which products contribute the most sales?
* Which states generate the highest revenue?
* Are there seasonal sales patterns?
* Which categories are growing fastest?

⸻

Customer Analytics

* Who are the highest-value customers?
* Which customers purchase repeatedly?
* What is the estimated Customer Lifetime Value?
* What customer segments exist?
* What is the repeat purchase rate?

⸻

Product Analytics

* Which products perform best?
* Which products receive poor reviews?
* Which categories have the highest ratings?
* Which products generate the highest revenue?

⸻

Seller Analytics

* Which sellers generate the highest revenue?
* Which sellers process the most orders?
* Which sellers receive the highest ratings?
* Which sellers experience delivery delays?

⸻

Operations Analytics

* What is the average delivery time?
* Which regions experience delivery delays?
* Does delivery speed influence customer ratings?
* How much freight cost is incurred?

⸻

Payment Analytics

* Which payment methods are most popular?
* How many installment payments are used?
* Does payment method influence order value?

⸻

Review Analytics

* What is the average customer rating?
* Which product categories receive poor reviews?
* What themes appear in negative reviews?
* Does delivery performance affect customer satisfaction?

⸻

KPIs

Sales

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value
* Monthly Revenue Growth

Customers

* Total Customers
* Repeat Purchase Rate
* Customer Lifetime Value
* RFM Segments

Products

* Product Revenue
* Category Revenue
* Product Ratings

Sellers

* Seller Revenue
* Seller Rating
* Seller Order Count

Operations

* Delivery Time
* Freight Cost
* Delayed Delivery Rate

Reviews

* Average Rating
* Positive Review %
* Negative Review %
