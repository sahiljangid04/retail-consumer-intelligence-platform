# Business Problem

## Overview

Olist is one of Brazil's largest e-commerce marketplaces, connecting thousands of independent sellers with customers across the country.

As the platform has grown, data has been generated across multiple operational systems including customer management, orders, products, payments, logistics, and customer reviews. Although the data exists, it has not been transformed into meaningful business insights for decision-makers.

The objective of this project is to design a **Retail Consumer Intelligence Platform** that converts raw transactional data into actionable insights through Python, SQL, and Power BI.

---

# Business Context

The executive team wants a centralized analytics solution capable of monitoring the entire customer journey.

Customer Journey

```
Customer
    │
Places Order
    │
Product Purchased
    │
Seller Processes Order
    │
Payment Completed
    │
Shipment Delivered
    │
Customer Leaves Review
```

Understanding each stage of this journey enables the business to identify opportunities for revenue growth, operational improvements, and better customer experiences.

---

# Business Objectives

The project aims to:

- Analyze sales performance.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate seller performance.
- Measure logistics efficiency.
- Improve customer satisfaction.
- Identify business growth opportunities.
- Build executive dashboards for data-driven decision making.
