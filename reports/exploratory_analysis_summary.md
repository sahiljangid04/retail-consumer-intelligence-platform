# Exploratory Analysis Summary

## Overview

This report summarizes the findings obtained during the Exploratory Data Analysis phase of the Retail Consumer Intelligence Platform.

The analysis was conducted using engineered business datasets and focuses on identifying trends, customer behaviour, operational performance, and revenue drivers.

---

# Key Findings

## Sales

- Revenue increased steadily across the analysis period.
- Quarter 2 generated the highest sales.
- Weekday purchases exceeded weekend purchases.
- Peak purchase activity occurred during afternoon and evening hours.

---

## Customers

- Most customers made only one purchase.
- Repeat customers account for a smaller portion of the customer base.
- Customer spending varies considerably across regions.

---

## Products

- Revenue is concentrated within a limited number of product categories.
- Health & Beauty, Watches & Gifts, and Bed & Bath products generated the highest revenue.

---

## Sellers

- A relatively small group of sellers generated a significant share of total marketplace revenue.
- Seller performance varies substantially across the platform.

---

## Payments

- Credit Card is the dominant payment method.
- Alternative payment methods contribute a much smaller share of transactions.

---

## Operations

- Most orders were successfully delivered.
- Average delivery time is approximately twelve days.
- Longer delivery delays are associated with lower customer review scores.

---

## Statistical Findings

Correlation analysis identified:

- Strong relationship between shipping time and delivery time.
- Moderate relationship between delivery delay and delivery duration.
- Weak negative relationship between review score and delivery delay.
- Strong positive relationship between price and payment value.

---

# Business Recommendations

## Sales

- Prioritize marketing campaigns during Quarter 2.
- Increase promotional activity during weekends.

## Customer

- Launch loyalty programs to improve repeat purchases.
- Focus retention efforts on high-value customers.

## Product

- Prioritize inventory for top-performing product categories.
- Review underperforming categories for optimization.

## Sellers

- Develop incentive programs for high-performing sellers.
- Monitor low-performing sellers through operational KPIs.

## Operations

- Reduce delivery delays through logistics optimization.
- Track delivery performance continuously using operational dashboards.

## Payment

- Continue optimizing credit card payment experience.
- Promote installment payment options for higher-value purchases.

---

# Conclusion

The exploratory analysis validated the engineered features and revealed clear business patterns across sales, customers, products, sellers, payments, and logistics.

These insights provide the analytical foundation for Customer Intelligence, Power BI dashboard development, and Predictive Analytics in subsequent project phases.
